About Zargovis

Zargovis Primary School was founded on a singular belief: every child deserves an education that sees them fully — their curiosity, their creativity, their emotions, and their boundless potential. We are a forward-thinking primary school that fuses the best of human teaching with the power of artificial intelligence, delivering an education that is deeply personal, joyfully engaging, and genuinely transformative.

Our school serves pupils from Nursery 1 through Primary 6, with carefully designed learning environments for each stage of childhood. We do not believe in one-size-fits-all education. Instead, we use adaptive AI tools to tailor content, pacing, and challenge level to each child — while our dedicated teachers provide the warmth, mentorship, and human connection that technology can never replace.

We are committed to raising children who are not only academically strong, but emotionally intelligent, technologically fluent, and ready to contribute positively to Nigeria and the world beyond.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Curiosity

We celebrate questions more than answers. Every child's natural wonder is treated as a gift to be nurtured, not a distraction to be silenced.

Inclusion

Every learner belongs here. We design our school to welcome every background, ability level, and learning style with equal warmth and opportunity.

Excellence

We hold high expectations — not as pressure, but as belief. We believe every child is capable of exceptional things when given the right support.

Growth Mindset

We teach children that effort, persistence, and learning from mistakes are the true measures of intelligence — not just right answers on a test.

Global Citizenship

Our pupils learn to think beyond their classroom — about the environment, other cultures, and their responsibility to the world around them.

Safeguarding

Child safety and well-being is non-negotiable. Every staff member, policy, and environment is designed to ensure every child is safe, seen, and valued.
